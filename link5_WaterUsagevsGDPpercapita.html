Water Usage vs. GDP

The visualization for comparison of water usage per capita to GDP per capita is created by using BubbleChart. The following graph demonstrates the comparison of water usage per capita to GDP per capita in 2006. The horizontal axis represents water usage per capita and the verticle axis represents GDP per capita. The key point of this graph is that there is no positive relationship between GDP and water usage per capita. For example, Germany has relatively higher GDP per capita, it does not show higher water usage per capita. Other five years show similar pattern.
