Water Treatment and Usage

The visualization for comparison of water usage per capita to government spending on environmental protection per capita is created in Tableau. The left graph is the comparison of water usage per capita to government spending on environmental protection per capita in 2006. The blue rectangular bars represent water usage per capita and the pink rectangular bars represent government spending on environmental protection per capita. Two rectangular bars are stacked for each country. The key point of this graph is that Japan has the highest environmental spending while relatively smaller water usage per capita. Other five years show similar pattern.
