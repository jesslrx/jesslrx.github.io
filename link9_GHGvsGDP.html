Air Pollution

The visualization for comparison of GHG emissions per capita and GDP per capita is created in Tableau. The left graph is the comparison of GHG emissions per capita and GDP per capita in 2006. The blue rectangular bars represent GDP per capita and the pink rectangular bars represent GHG emissions per capita. Two separate bar charts are created as GDP and GHG emissions have values from different dimensions. The graph shows that there is no positive relationship between GDP and GHG emissions. For example, Australia has the highest amount of GHG emission per capita, it does not show highest GDP per capita.
